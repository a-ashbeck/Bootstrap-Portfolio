About Me

Alan is a 7 year veteran in the field of behavioral interventions, and has been working with mobile and web-based applications for the last 2 years. His goals are to push the envelope of on these interventions by creating eHealth and mHealth tech to help further the science of behavioral intervention, and to help individuals struggling with mental illness. He recently enrolled in a coding boot camp at Northwestern University in order to further his career as a developer of behavioral science tech products.

Alan hails from Chicago, Illinois, and currently works at Northwestern University in the Center for Behavioral Intervention Technologies. He acquired his B.A. in Applied Psychology in 2011 from the University of Illinois at Chicago. In his spare time he enjoys playing guitar, deadlifting, and solving puzzles and brain teasers.
